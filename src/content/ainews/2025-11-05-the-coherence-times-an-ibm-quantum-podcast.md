## The Coherence Times: IBM's New Podcast Explores Quantum Computing Fundamentals and Innovations

### Introduction
IBM has launched *The Coherence Times*, a weekly podcast hosted by IBM Quantum editor-in-chief Ryan Mandelbaum, aimed at demystifying quantum computing for enthusiasts and professionals. The series focuses on foundational concepts, breakthroughs, and the human stories behind quantum technology, with episodes released every other Tuesday at 7:00 a.m. Eastern Time.

### Key Themes and Structure
- **Educational Focus**:  
  - Designed for all levels of quantum curiosity, from students to industry experts.  
  - Season 1 emphasizes core concepts in quantum hardware, software, and algorithms.  
  - Subsequent seasons delve into cutting-edge research and innovations.  

- **Format and Accessibility**:  
  - Episodes are available on Spotify, Apple Podcasts, and IBM Research’s YouTube channel.  
  - Conversations with quantum researchers, developers, and thought leaders highlight both technical challenges and human ingenuity.  

- **Schedule and Availability**:  
  - New episodes released biweekly on Tuesdays.  
  - Trailer and first episodes accessible via the provided URL.  

### Impact and Purpose
- **Democratizing Quantum Knowledge**:  
  - Breaks down complex topics into digestible, engaging content.  
  - Bridges the gap between academic research and practical applications.  

- **Community Engagement**:  
  - Encourages dialogue between quantum professionals and the public.  
  - Highlights global efforts to advance quantum computing, including IBM’s role in the field.  

### Target Audience
- **Quantum Enthusiasts**: Casual learners seeking foundational knowledge.  
- **Industry Professionals**: Researchers and developers looking for insights into hardware/software advancements.  
- **Academia**: Educators and students interested in quantum theory and applications.
