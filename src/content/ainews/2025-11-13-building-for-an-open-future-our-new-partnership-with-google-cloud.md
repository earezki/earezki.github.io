## Building for an Open Future - our new partnership with Google Cloud

Hugging Face and Google Cloud announced a strategic partnership to enable companies to build AI with open models, citing a 10x growth in Hugging Face usage by Google Cloud customers over three years. The collaboration includes a CDN Gateway to reduce model download times by caching repositories directly on Google Cloud.

### Why This Matters
Deploying open models at scale requires balancing idealized system performance with real-world constraints like latency and cost. Without optimizations like the CDN Gateway, model download times and supply chain robustness become bottlenecks. Google Cloud’s infrastructure and Hugging Face’s model library address these challenges, reducing friction for enterprises.
