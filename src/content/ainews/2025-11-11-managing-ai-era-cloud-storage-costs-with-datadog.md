## Managing AI-era cloud storage costs with Datadog

Datadog launches Storage Management to tackle AI-era cloud storage costs, which now rank as the third-largest expense for AI product development. The tool integrates cost, usage, and metadata into a unified dashboard for S3 and multi-cloud environments.

### Why This Matters
Enterprises face a critical gap between ideal cloud storage models and technical reality: untracked data growth and inefficient policies lead to runaway costs. Without visibility, storage expenses—now exceeding model training and inference—can consume 30%+ of AI budgets. Datadog’s solution addresses this by automating anomaly detection and policy enforcement across billions of objects, reducing the risk of uncontrolled spending in multi-cloud environments.

### Key Insights
- "Storage and processing represent the third-largest cost for AI products, surpassing model training and inference" (Datadog, 2025)
- "Sagas over ACID for e-commerce": Lifecycle policies replace rigid ACID transactions for managing temporary AI data
- "Temporal used by Stripe, Coinbase": Workflow orchestration tools for automated storage governance

### Practical Applications
- **Use Case**: Multi-cloud enterprises monitoring 100M+ objects across S3, GCS, and Azure Blob Storage
- **Pitfall**: Siloed finance/IT teams failing to align storage policies, leading to 40%+ redundant data retention
