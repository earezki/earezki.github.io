## Dynamic Attack Surface Reduction (DASR): A Smarter Way to Stay Safe

Join Bitdefender and The Hacker News for a 2025 webinar on Dynamic Attack Surface Reduction (DASR), a method that automates risk closure before attackers identify vulnerabilities. Traditional tools generate endless alerts but fail to fix them, leaving organizations exposed to evolving threats.

### Why This Matters
Traditional security scans report vulnerabilities but do not address them in real time, leaving attack surfaces open as new apps, cloud systems, and misconfigurations constantly expand risks. Attackers exploit this delay, with 82% of breaches involving unpatched vulnerabilities (Ponemon, 2024). DASR shifts from reactive scanning to proactive automation, reducing exposure by closing gaps as they emerge.

### Key Insights
- "82% of breaches involve unpatched vulnerabilities, 2024 (Ponemon)"
- "Sagas over ACID for e-commerce": DASR prioritizes continuous risk reduction over static compliance checks
- "PHASR system used by Bitdefender for real-time attack surface monitoring"

### Practical Applications
- **Use Case**: Bitdefender's PHASR system automatically closes misconfigured cloud endpoints
- **Pitfall**: Relying on periodic scans creates windows of exposure between assessments
