## Securing Cloud Workloads and Infrastructure: Balancing Innovation with Identity and Access Control

CyberArk is hosting a webinar on November 2025 focusing on cloud security, driven by the increasing complexity of multi-cloud environments and the escalating threat of identity-based attacks. The event features experts Przemek Dybowski and Josh Kirkwood, offering practical guidance for businesses navigating these challenges.

### Why This Matters
Ideal security models assume perfect configuration and constant vigilance, but real-world cloud deployments often suffer from misconfigurations and vulnerabilities in identity and access management. A single compromised credential can lead to significant data breaches, regulatory fines, and reputational damage; the average cost of a data breach in 2023 exceeded $4.45 million.

### Key Insights
- **Increasing Attack Surface**: Cloud adoption expands the attack surface, requiring robust identity and access controls.
- **Multi-Cloud Complexity**: Managing security across multiple cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, GCP) introduces operational overhead and potential inconsistencies.
- **CyberArk Expertise**: CyberArk provides solutions for privileged access management, helping organizations secure their most critical assets.

### Practical Applications
- **Financial Institutions**: Employing strong access controls to meet stringent regulatory requirements while maintaining operational agility. 
- **Pitfall**: Overly permissive access policies leading to lateral movement by attackers within the cloud environment.
