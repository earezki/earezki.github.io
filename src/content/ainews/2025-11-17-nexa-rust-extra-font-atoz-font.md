## Nexa Rust Extra, designed by Fontfabric, falls under the Display, Script category & Nexa Rust font family

Nexa Rust Extra, created by Fontfabric, is a Display/Script font optimized for visual appeal in design projects. It balances decorative elements with readability, making it ideal for branding and promotional materials.

### Why This Matters
Fonts like Nexa Rust Extra highlight the tension between aesthetic design and functional usability. While Display fonts prioritize visual impact, they risk compromising legibility in smaller sizes or dense text. This tradeoff is critical for engineers and designers selecting typefaces for multi-platform applications, where scalability and accessibility must align with stylistic goals.
