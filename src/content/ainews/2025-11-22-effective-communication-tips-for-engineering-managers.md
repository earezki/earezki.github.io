## Effective Communication Tips for Engineering Managers

Diamantino Almeida, a software engineering leader, outlines 10 critical communication strategies for managers. One hard fact: poor communication costs organizations up to 15% of their annual revenue in delays and errors.

### Why This Matters
Technical teams often assume clarity is inherent, but misaligned expectations between engineers, stakeholders, and cross-functional partners create friction. Ideal models assume perfect information symmetry, yet real-world projects face ambiguity, jargon barriers, and unspoken assumptions. For example, a 2023 study found that 68% of engineering delays stem from unclear requirements or feedback loops.
