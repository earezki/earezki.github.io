## AI Agent Builders to Explore for Business Ops

AI agents are evolving beyond simple LLM prompts to autonomously complete tasks, call APIs, and execute end-to-end workflows. These agents represent a shift from AI assisting thought to AI performing work, with a Cloudera survey in April 2025 finding that 96% of enterprise IT leaders plan to expand their use of AI agents over the next 12 months.

### Why This Matters
Current AI models often require extensive coding and infrastructure management, creating a barrier to entry for many businesses. Ideal models promise seamless automation, but the reality is complex integration and maintenance costs. Failure to implement effective AI agents can result in missed automation opportunities and continued reliance on expensive, manual processes.
