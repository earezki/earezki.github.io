### Why This Matters
Biotech valuations are heavily driven by clinical catalysts rather than current financials, especially in pre-commercial companies. With Jyong Biotech’s trial enrollment completed ahead of schedule and data analysis now underway, the market is positioned for a near-term information update that could significantly re-rate the stock, despite ongoing cash burn.

- **Negative Free Cash Flow**: Sustained cash outflows due to clinical development, typical for early-stage biotechs.
- **News Impact**: Completion of 700+ patient enrollment in Phase II trial for MCS-8 signals strong operational execution and near-term data readout potential.
