## AMZN – Increase in Days/Weeks

Amazon generated $32.88 billion in free cash flow over the last twelve months, signaling robust operational strength, just as early holiday shopping data reveals a 22% YoY surge in Amazon platform sales during Black Friday weekend.

### Why This Matters
Amazon’s cash generation ability—despite high capital expenditures—reflects pricing power and operating efficiency, particularly in AWS and Prime ecosystems. With the 2025 holiday season off to a stronger-than-expected start and cloud utilization spiking due to AI workloads, near-term revenue visibility has improved significantly, making the stock poised for upward momentum in the next few weeks.

- **Free Cash Flow (TTM)**: $32.88 billion, up from $25.1 billion in prior year, indicating improved liquidity and reinvestment capacity.
