## ONDS – increase in Days/Weeks

Ondas Holdings reports deeply negative free cash flow of $-35.2M and a debt/equity ratio of 3.688, reflecting significant financial strain. However, recent news confirms a new multi-year agreement with a major U.S. rail operator to deploy its FullMAX private wireless network, enhancing revenue visibility.

### Why This Matters
The company’s core financials remain weak, with no profitability and substantial cash burn, but its technology is gaining traction in mission-critical industrial sectors where reliable connectivity is paramount. The rail deal—valued at over $20 million in initial committed spending—validates product-market fit and comes amid increasing regulatory support for private wireless infrastructure, making this a pivotal moment for near-term revenue acceleration despite balance sheet risks.

### Key Insights
- **Free Cash Flow (TTM)**: $-35.2M — ongoing cash burn raises sustainability concerns without dilution or new financing
