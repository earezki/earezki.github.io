## BTDG – increase in Days/Weeks

### Why This Matters
The dramatic improvement in gross margin—from 4.5% to 24.1%—reflects pricing power and operational efficiency in Bitdeer’s core mining and HPC business, even as net losses remain elevated due to non-cash derivative charges. With the market now focusing on revenue quality and future AI cloud monetization—targeting a $2 billion annualized run-rate by 2026—investors are likely to overlook near-term profitability in favor of growth trajectory and balance sheet stabilization, especially given the concurrent reduction in high-cost debt.
