## JAZZ – increase in Days/Weeks

Jazz Pharmaceuticals generated $1.35B in free cash flow over the past year, signaling robust operational efficiency despite high leverage. On November 17, 2025, the company announced positive topline results from its Phase 3 HERIZON-GEA-01 trial, showing statistically significant improvements in both progression-free and overall survival with Ziihera in HER2-positive gastroesophageal adenocarcinoma.

### Why This Matters
The combination of strong cash generation and a potentially practice-changing clinical result creates a powerful catalyst for re-rating; Ziihera’s potential to become the new first-line standard of care in metastatic GEA—regardless of PD-L1 status—positions Jazz for substantial revenue growth, especially given the large addressable market in gastroesophageal cancers. With the stock already reacting sharply upward, momentum is likely to persist as investors reassess long-term earnings power.
