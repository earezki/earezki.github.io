## TERN – increase in Days/Weeks

This financial commitment coincides with the release of positive Phase 1 CML data for TERN-701 and an upcoming oral presentation at ASH 2025, serving as a key near-term catalyst.

### Why This Matters
The biotech market prices stocks on binary clinical outcomes, not profitability, and Terns’ decision to streamline its pipeline toward oncology—while preserving cash into 2028—positions TERN-701 as a high-upside asset. With institutional holders like Orbimed and Vivo Capital signaling confidence, and management de-risking by partnering out metabolic programs, capital is now focused on a single, potentially best-in-disease oncology candidate, amplifying the market impact of upcoming data.
