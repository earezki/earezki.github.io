## NUVL – Increase in Days/Weeks

Nuvalent is burning $185 million in free cash flow annually, yet reporting a 31% objective response rate in advanced ALK-positive NSCLC and an 86% response rate in first-line patients. The stock surged 16% intraday on November 17 following these results, coinciding with a $500 million public offering.

### Why This Matters
Biotech valuations hinge on clinical milestones, not profitability—Nuvalent’s robust trial data for neladalkib (NVL-655) validates its scientific differentiation and regulatory pathway, justifying near-term re-rating despite cash burn. With plans for a pre-NDA meeting with the FDA, the market is pricing in accelerated approval potential, making this a classic sentiment-driven momentum event in the biotech sector.
