## TERN – increase in Days/Weeks

Terns Pharmaceuticals reported a deepening net loss of $21.9 million in Q3 2024, reflecting ongoing negative free cash flow typical of clinical-stage biotechs. However, the company’s cash runway now extends into 2028 following a $172.7 million equity raise, with critical interim Phase 1 data for its CML drug TERN-701 expected in early December 2024.

### Why This Matters
The combination of extended financial runway and proximity to a high-impact clinical readout fundamentally shifts investor risk perception: while the company remains unprofitable and cash-burning, the near-zero probability of near-term dilution removes a key overhang, allowing the market to focus purely on the value of upcoming data. With TERN-701’s interim results due in early December—just days away—the stock is positioned for a potential catalyst-driven rally, especially given the high unmet need in resistant CML and the drug’s novel allosteric mechanism.
