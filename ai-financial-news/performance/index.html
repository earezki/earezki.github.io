Methodology

Prediction Timeframe: Each prediction is validated 14 days after publication.

Success Criteria: A prediction is marked correct if the actual price movement direction (increase/decrease) matches the predicted direction, regardless of magnitude.

Price Source: Historical prices are fetched from Yahoo Finance. If the exact target date falls on a weekend or holiday, the closest trading day is used.

Confidence Calibration: Ideally, predictions with 7/10 confidence should be correct ~70% of the time. This section helps track if our confidence scores are well-calibrated.
